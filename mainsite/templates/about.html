Our Story

Crafty Coders Apps started as a passion project, something fun to do during the COVID-19 lockdown. It went from focusing on apps to help crafters to games designed for kids. But not just kids. Jewish kids. Crafty Coders Apps strives to bring games to help kids learn Hebrew, to learn about their Jewish heritage. We started with the Alef-Bais, the most fundamental part. We hope to expand our range in the future!
